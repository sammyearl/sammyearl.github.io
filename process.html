How We Work

Our Process

Every project begins with listening. We take the time to understand your space, your lifestyle, and what you're hoping for — then we get to work.

1

Initial Consultation

We start with either a Zoom call or an in-person site visit to see your outdoor space and listen to what you're hoping for. This is where we get to know you, your property, and your vision.

2

Site Survey & Design

We take measurements and photographs of your site, then begin the design phase. Working from your brief and the site's unique conditions, we develop and refine a landscape concept drawing that accounts for local site conditions, spaces to "be" and spaces to "live," and a preliminary plant palette.

3

Design Review

Once the drawing is ready, we schedule a meeting to walk through the ideas together and incorporate any adjustments. We refine until the design feels right.

Design fee: Calculated on an hourly basis, typically ranging from $1,500 to $2,500 depending on the scope and complexity of the project.
4

Build & Implementation

We are a design-build company and love to bring our designs to life. If you're happy with the design, we can break the project into phases that work with your budget and schedule, and price out options based on subcontractor availability and plant material.

Construction fee: We act as General Contractor on the project and charge a 20% construction and supervision fee, with some variability depending on the specific project.
